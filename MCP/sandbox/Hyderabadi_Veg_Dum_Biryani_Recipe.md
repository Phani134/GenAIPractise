# Hyderabadi Style Veg Dum Biryani Recipe

## Ingredients

### For Rice:
- 2 cups Basmati rice
- 4 cups water
- 1 bay leaf
- 4-5 cloves
- 2-3 cardamom pods
- 1 small cinnamon stick
- Salt to taste

### For Vegetable Gravy:
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes, cauliflower)
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup yogurt, whisked
- 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
- 2 green chilies, slit
- 1/2 cup mint leaves
- 1/2 cup coriander leaves
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 cup fried onions
- 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
- Salt to taste

### For Layering:
- A few saffron strands soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk
- 2 tbsp ghee

### Whole Spices for Dum:
- 1 bay leaf
- 4-5 cloves
- 2-3 cardamom pods
- 1 small cinnamon stick

## Instructions

### Preparing the Rice:
1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes.
2. Boil water with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon) and salt.
3. Add soaked rice to boiling water and cook until 70% done.
4. Drain the water and keep the rice aside.

### Preparing the Vegetable Gravy:
1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add ginger garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for 2 minutes.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
5. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt.
6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
7. Add whisked yogurt, half of the mint and coriander leaves, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
8. Add garam masala and mix well.

### Assembling the Biryani:
1. In a heavy bottom pot, spread a layer of vegetable gravy.
2. Add a layer of rice on top of the gravy.
3. Sprinkle fried onions, remaining mint and coriander leaves, saffron milk, and ghee.
4. Repeat the layering if ingredients remain, ending with a rice layer.
5. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal with dough to trap the steam.
6. Cook on very low heat (dum) for 30-40 minutes.

### Serving:
- Gently fluff the biryani and serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your flavorful and aromatic Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani!
