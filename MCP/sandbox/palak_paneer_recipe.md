# Palak Paneer Recipe

## Ingredients
- 250 grams paneer (cubed)
- 500 grams fresh spinach (palak), washed and chopped
- 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1-inch ginger piece, minced
- 2 green chilies, chopped
- 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
- 1/4 cup fresh cream (optional)
- Salt to taste
- Water as needed

## Instructions
1. Blanch the spinach: Boil water in a large pot, add the chopped spinach for 2 minutes. Drain and immediately transfer to ice-cold water. Drain again and blend into a smooth puree. Set aside.
2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. When they crackle, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating from the masala.
5. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
6. Add the spinach puree and mix. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
7. Add garam masala and stir.
8. Add cubed paneer and gently mix to coat the paneer with the spinach gravy.
9. Add fresh cream if using and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
10. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Palak Paneer!
